Small Baby Shower Ideas 2025

If you’re expecting a baby in 2025, you’re probably already thinking about how to celebrate. A baby shower is a great way to celebrate the upcoming arrival of your little one and to stock up on all the essentials you’ll need.

Here are a few small baby shower ideas for 2025:

  • Virtual baby shower: If you’re not able to have a traditional baby shower in person, you could host a virtual baby shower instead. This is a great option for guests who live far away or who have busy schedules. You can use a video conferencing platform like Zoom or Google Meet to host your virtual baby shower.
  • Outdoor baby shower: If you’re having a baby in the spring or summer, you could host an outdoor baby shower. This is a great way to enjoy the warm weather and to get some fresh air. You could have your baby shower at a park, a backyard, or even a beach.
  • Co-ed baby shower: If you want to include your partner and other male friends and family members in the celebration, you could host a co-ed baby shower. This is a great way to make everyone feel included and to celebrate the upcoming arrival of your baby together.
  • Diaper party: If you’re looking for a more practical baby shower gift, you could host a diaper party. Guests can bring diapers, wipes, and other baby essentials instead of traditional gifts. This is a great way to stock up on all the supplies you’ll need for the first few months with your new baby.
  • Baby sprinkle: If you’re having a second or third baby, you could host a baby sprinkle instead of a traditional baby shower. This is a smaller, more intimate gathering that is focused on celebrating the upcoming arrival of your new baby and providing you with a few essential items.

No matter what type of baby shower you choose, make sure that it is something that you and your guests will enjoy. The most important thing is to celebrate the upcoming arrival of your little one and to have some fun.

Here are a few additional tips for planning a small baby shower:

  • Keep it simple. Don’t try to do too much. A small baby shower should be relaxed and intimate.
  • Choose a theme. This will help you to plan the decorations, food, and activities for your baby shower.
  • Send out invitations early. Give your guests plenty of time to RSVP.
  • Set up a gift registry. This will help your guests to choose gifts that you need and want.
  • Have fun! This is a special time in your life. Enjoy the celebration and the company of your loved ones.
